Meet the Characters

Get to know the main characters of Persona 5 and discover their unique abilities and roles in the game.

Joker

Joker is the protagonist of Persona 5. He is a high school student who becomes a Phantom Thief and uses his Persona, Arsene, to fight against corruption.

Morgana

Morgana is a mysterious cat-like creature who joins Joker and the other Phantom Thieves. He serves as their guide and provides valuable advice during their missions.

Ryuji Sakamoto

Ryuji is one of Joker's classmates and a member of the Phantom Thieves. He has a rebellious personality and his Persona, Captain Kidd, specializes in physical attacks.

Ann Takamaki

Ann is a popular student at Shujin Academy and a member of the Phantom Thieves. Her Persona, Carmen, excels in fire-based attacks and support skills.

Yusuke Kitagawa

Yusuke is an aspiring artist and a member of the Phantom Thieves. His Persona, Goemon, specializes in ice-based attacks and has high agility.

Makoto Niijima

Makoto is the student council president at Shujin Academy and a member of the Phantom Thieves. Her Persona, Johanna, excels in nuclear-based attacks and defense.

Haru Okumura

Haru is a wealthy heiress and a member of the Phantom Thieves. Her Persona, Milady, excels in psychic-based attacks and healing abilities.

Futaba Sakura

Futaba is a genius hacker and a member of the Phantom Thieves. Her Persona, Necronomicon, specializes in electric-based attacks and support skills.
